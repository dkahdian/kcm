Family-Level Liftability Summary (2026-04-14)

This file documents what union-level (e.g. OBDD) results could be passed to the family-lefel (e.g. OBDD_<).

Most results work as expected under our definition of succintness for language families. A few do not. See below.

Liftable to fixed-parameter family targets (supported):
1) OBDD_< -> dec-SDNNF_T (poly)
2) uOBDD_< -> d-SDNNF_T (poly)
3) nOBDD_< -> SDNNF_T (poly)
4) dec-SDNNF_T -> d-SDNNF_T (poly, subset relation)
5) d-SDNNF_T -> SDNNF_T (poly, subset relation)

Not liftable to fixed-parameter family targets from the current citations:
1) SDNNF_T ~> nOBDD_< (quasi)
2) d-SDNNF_T ~> uOBDD_< (quasi)
3) dec-SDNNF_T ~> OBDD_< (quasi)

Reason these three are not established:
- The cited simulation selects an output order pi(D,T) that depends on the input circuit D, not only on T.
- Therefore, one fixed target order per source family member is not guaranteed.
